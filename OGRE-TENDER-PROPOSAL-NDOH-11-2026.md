## 11. FAQ — INTERNATIONAL COMPANY STATUS

**Q: Can a foreign-registered company bid alone?**
A: Yes, but scores 0/10 on B-BBEE. Nearly impossible to win against Level 1 SA companies on 90/10 system unless your price is significantly below market.

**Q: What is a commission company?**
A: A company that earns a commission or fee for services rendered, rather than selling products directly. OGRE can act as an international commission agent providing technology services to a SA prime contractor.

**Q: Does OGRE need a South African bank account?**
A: For contract payments, yes. However, international wire transfers are possible. Best resolved via the SA partner's banking.

**Q: What about the National Industrial Participation (NIP) programme?**
A: Only applies if imported content exceeds US$10M. OGRE's AI agent services are largely SA-delivered, so NIP is unlikely to trigger. Confirm with DTI if in doubt.

**Q: Can OGRE submit a bid without attending the compulsory briefing?**
A: The briefing was July 2 — bids can still be submitted even if you didn't attend, but you miss critical technical clarifications published at the briefing.
