# Purpose — Dark Factory Wiki

## Why This Wiki Exists

This wiki is the **persistent memory** for [[Cipher Tr@ce]] and the Dark Factory. It ensures that knowledge built across sessions — client details, engineering decisions, partnership contacts, revenue tracking — is never lost and always instantly accessible.

The alternative is forgetting. We don't forget.

---

## Key Questions This Wiki Must Always Be Able to Answer

### Clients & Revenue
- Who are our clients and what VMs do they have?
- What proposals are out and what's the expected revenue?
- What proposals are pending and what's blocking them?
- What did we promise clients and when did we promise it?

### Engineering
- What is the Dark Factory stack and how does it work?
- What skills are installed on each VM?
- What is the PRD intake workflow?
- What blockers exist in the build pipeline?

### Partnerships
- Who are our active partners and what's the status?
- What did Pharmasyntez agree to? What are the terms?
- What partnerships are we actively pursuing?

### Operations
- What is every agent doing right now?
- What emails have been sent and what's pending?
- What does Tumelo need to act on today?

## What Gets Added

✅ Client names, VMs, proposals, pricing, status  
✅ Engineering decisions and why they were made  
✅ Partnership terms, contacts, next steps  
✅ Revenue pipeline with amounts and stages  
✅ Skills installed, what they do, how to use them  
✅ Blockers with owners and ETA

## What Gets Archived

❌ Raw session logs (→ `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`)  
❌ Temporary research that never became actionable  
❌ Dead deal notes (→ `archive/dead-deals.md`)  
❌ Test outputs and experimental code (→ repo, not wiki)

---

## How Knowledge Flows

```
Session → memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md (raw log)
    ↓ (weekly review)
MEMORY.md + wiki/ (curated knowledge)
    ↓
Every future session queries wiki/ first
    ↓
New knowledge → added to wiki on the spot
```
