1. About OGRE Computer

OGRE Computer is an African-owned AI infrastructure company operating under the Studex Group. We design, build, and manage sovereign AI systems — GPU infrastructure, autonomous agent platforms, and local LLM deployments — for governments, enterprises, and organisations that cannot trust their data to foreign hyperscalers.

Our core thesis: Africa needs African-owned AI infrastructure. Not Alibaba Cloud. Not Microsoft Azure. Not Google AWS. OGRE fills this gap — providing the complete stack from hardware procurement through to agent orchestration and managed services, all hosted within the continent under African ownership and control.

OGRE is registered in South Africa with active operations across South Africa, Nigeria, and Kenya — the three largest AI procurement markets on the continent. We are actively registered and pursuing government AI tenders across all three jurisdictions.
