### StudEx Wild Life — "The World's First Tokenized Wildlife Conservation"
**Founded:** 2017 | **Partners:** IBM · Cardano ($ADA) | **Status:** ACTIVE

**The concept:**
A blockchain-based platform that allows global participants to become "virtual farmers" — purchasing fractional or full ownership of endangered wildlife via NFTs (ERC-721) or tokens (ERC-20). The funds go directly back into conservation.

**How it works (from Forbes Africa, April 2019):**
1. IBM provides the IoT infrastructure — sensors on animals track location, heart rate, activity
2. Cardano blockchain records all tracking data on-chain (immutable, transparent)
3. Individuals buy NFTs representing ownership of specific animals
4. Token holders can sell/trade the NFTs (like CryptoKitties)
5. All funds flow back to conservation projects protecting the animals

**Why IBM + Cardano:**
- IBM: IoT hardware, cloud infrastructure, enterprise credibility
- Cardano: Proof-of-stake blockchain, Africa-first positioning, $ADA token utility

**Tumelo's exact quote (Forbes, 2019):**
*"I wanted to create a digital version of my family's farm, which evolved into a wildlife conservation movement."*

**The scale of ambition:**
- Launch Africa's first blockchain-focused university
- Blockchain accelerator for young SA entrepreneurs
- Tokenize all of Africa's endangered species

**Messari report confirmation (2025):**
