## THE 5-QUESTION INTAKE (MANDATORY)

Every client submission MUST answer these 5 questions before we send a proposal.
If they can't answer all 5, we schedule a 30-minute scoping call first.

```
□ WHO is the end user? (role, tech level, daily frustrations)
□ WHAT do they need to accomplish? (plain language, not features)
□ WHAT data is involved? (names, emails, medical, financial?)
□ WHERE must data live? (SA VM only / any / client's choice)
□ WHAT is the budget and timeline? (R___ / ___weeks / who signs?)
```

**No budget = no proposal. Quote R500K minimum.**

## POPIA COMPLEXITY SCORE

Before writing the proposal, score the POPIA complexity:

```
Data type: (circle one)
  0 = No personal data (public info only)
  5 = Business contacts (name, email, company)
  10 = Client personal data (phone, address)
  20 = Special personal data (medical, financial, biometric, IDs)

Data volume: (circle one)
  0 = < 100 records
  5 = 100–1,000 records
  10 = 1,000–10,000 records
  20 = 10,000+ records

Third-party sharing: (circle one)
  0 = No third parties
  5 = One third party (e.g. bank, logistics)
  10 = Multiple third parties

TOTAL SCORE:
  0–10: Standard deployment — R0 POPIA surcharge
  11–20: Medium complexity — +R5,000 setup
  21–30: High complexity — +R15,000 setup + DPA mandatory
  31+:  Expert review required — +R25,000 + dedicated compliance officer
```

## ESCALATION RULES

**Escalate to Tier 3+ if:**
- Scope mentions "multiple departments" or "whole organisation"
- Budget is unclear or "whatever it costs"
- Timeline is under 2 weeks for Tier 2+ work
- Special personal data is involved (medical, HIV status, financial records)
- Client says "our lawyers will review the contract"
- More than 3 rounds of scoping questions

**Escalate to Tumelo if:**
- Deal is above R500K
- Government tender involved
- Media/press/public figure involved
- Multi-country deployment
- Client is a competitor or potential partner
