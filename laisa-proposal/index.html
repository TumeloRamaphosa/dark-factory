02 — BILLING & FINANCE

From Slow Invoicing to Instant, Automated Billing

Invoices generated the moment treatment is logged. EFT details sent automatically. Outstanding balances tracked daily. Follow-up sequences run without human input. Collections improve dramatically — patients pay faster when it's frictionless.

→ Invoice generated at treatment completion
→ EFT details + SnapScan sent via WhatsApp
→ 7/14/30-day auto-follow-up sequences
→ Real-time revenue dashboard
03 — PATIENT EXPERIENCE

From Paper Forms to Intelligent Digital Experience

Patients receive a WhatsApp message before their appointment with everything they need: directions, pre-treatment instructions, consent forms. After treatment: care instructions, follow-up schedule, satisfaction check. Every touchpoint elevated.

→ Digital consent forms — sign on WhatsApp
→ Pre-treatment instructions auto-sent
→ Post-treatment care sequence
→ Satisfaction survey — catch problems before they become reviews
