## Section 7: FAQ

### Q1: What exactly is a "StudEx Agent"?

A StudEx agent is an AI-powered virtual colleague that works inside your WhatsApp Business account (and other channels). It reads messages, responds to enquiries, sends quotes, follows up on leads, confirms appointments, and runs automations — all based on rules and workflows you configure. It's not a chatbot. It's not a button. It's an agent that thinks and acts on your behalf within your existing communication channels.

---

### Q2: Do my customers know they're talking to an AI?

Your agent can be configured to identify itself as an automated assistant, or it can operate transparently as "the team." Most of our SMB clients choose transparency — calling it an "automated assistant" — because it manages expectations without feeling cold or impersonal. You can also review and approve responses before they go out, or set the agent to only act on specific types of enquiries.

---

### Q3: How long does it take to set up?

Setup takes 20-30 minutes on a call with our team. We'll connect your WhatsApp Business account, configure your first agent with your business information, pricing, and workflows, and run a live test with you. After that call, your agent is live. The 14-day trial starts from your first live agent interaction — not from sign-up.

---

### Q4: What if the AI says something wrong?

Every agent action is logged in your Pixel Command Center. You can set confidence thresholds — below a certain score, the agent flags you for manual review before responding. You can also review and edit any response before it sends. We include a human-in-the-loop layer specifically for the businesses where accuracy matters most: legal, medical-adjacent, financial services. No black boxes.

---

### Q5: Is my data and customer information secure?

Yes. StudEx operates on enterprise-grade infrastructure with encrypted data storage, role-based access controls, and full compliance with POPIA (South Africa), NDPR (Nigeria), and data protection standards in Kenya. Your customer data is yours. We don't train our AI models on your business data. Your WhatsApp chat history is accessed only for the purpose of running your agent — never for any other purpose.

---

### Q6: Can I use StudEx if I'm not tech-savvy?

Absolutely. This was designed for non-technical African entrepreneurs. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use StudEx. Our onboarding team handles all the technical configuration on a call with you. The Command Center is designed to be read, not studied — live feeds, simple metrics, clear status indicators. No technical background required.

---

### Q7: What WhatsApp plan do I need?

You need WhatsApp Business App (free) or WhatsApp Business API (for higher volume). Our team will advise you on which is appropriate for your enquiry volume and help you set it up. There is no additional WhatsApp API cost for Starter and Growth plans. For Scale plan clients with very high volumes, there may be a separate Meta API fee — we'll tell you upfront if this applies.

---

### Q8: What happens if I want to cancel?

You can cancel at any time from your account settings, or by sending us a WhatsApp message. No cancellation fees. No notice period. No questions asked — though we will ask for feedback because we're always improving. If you cancel during your 14-day trial, you pay nothing. If you cancel after that, your billing stops at the end of your current paid month.
