How It Works

Patient sends a WhatsApp message, emails, or fills in a web form. Agent 01 receives it immediately.

The system scores the enquiry, flags urgency, logs the patient, and routes to the correct agent — automatically.

Appointment booked, voice note transcribed, invoice generated, consent logged, social post published — all done.

Everything is logged, audited, and ready for your review. Approve what needs your attention. The system handles the rest.
