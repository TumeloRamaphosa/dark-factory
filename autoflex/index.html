How it works

From visitor to converted
customer in one conversation

AutoFlex Pro uses Page-Agent's DOM-reading technology to understand your website's structure. When a visitor types a request, it reads the form, fills it, submits it, and notifies your team.

1

Install (2 lines of code)

Add our JS snippet to your website header. No backend changes. Works on any platform — WordPress, Webflow, custom.

2

AI reads your website

AutoFlex scans the DOM and learns your forms, buttons, and structure. It maps every field, option, and workflow automatically.

3

Visitor speaks naturally

"I want to book a dentist appointment for next Tuesday." AutoFlex understands intent, asks only missing info, skips the rest.

4

Form filled, CRM updated

AutoFlex completes the booking, submits it, updates your CRM, sends confirmation SMS/email, and notifies your team on Slack or WhatsApp.
