## What Is an International Commission Company?

An **International Commission Company (ICC)** is a foreign-registered company that provides **technology, services, and expertise** to a local company in another country — without needing to register locally. The ICC earns a **commission or fee** for its contribution, while the local company handles compliance, billing, and client relationships.

In OGRE's case: **OGRE Computer is the ICC.** The SA partner is the local company.

## Legal Requirements for OGRE as ICC

### OGRE Must Have:
| Requirement | Purpose | Status |
|------------|---------|--------|
| Company registration (any country) | Legal entity proof | ✅ OGRE already exists |
| Tax registration in home country | International tax compliance | ✅ |

### SA Partner Must Have:
| Requirement | Purpose | Status |
|------------|---------|--------|
| CIPC company registration | Legal bidder status | ❌ Must find partner |
| SARS Tax Clearance (TCS PIN) | Government payment eligibility | ❌ Partner must obtain |
| B-BBEE Certificate (Level 1-2) | Claim 10 preference points | ❌ Must find partner |
| Central Supplier Database (CSD) | Government supplier registry | ❌ Partner must register |
| Registered bank account (SA) | Receive government payments | ❌ Partner must have |

## The Service Agreement (Critical Document)

OGRE and the SA partner need a **signed Service Agreement** before bidding. Key clauses:

```

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2. Commission: 10% of all payments received from client
3. Payment terms: Within 30 days of SA partner receiving from client
4. OGRE delivers: AI agent stack, technical deployment, training automation
5. SA partner delivers: B-BBEE certificate, local registration, billing
6. Term: 18 months (matches tender contract)
7. Exit clauses: Either party can exit with 60 days notice
8. IP: OGRE retains ownership of AI agent technology
9. Confidentiality: Both parties keep commercial terms private
10. Dispute resolution: Arbitration in Johannesburg (or SA courts)
```

✅ **Scalable** — OGRE can partner with multiple SA companies across sectors.
✅ **Revenue generating NOW** — Start earning from day one of partnership.
✅ **Risk limited** — Only responsible for technology delivery, not local compliance.

## ICC vs Full SA Registration

| Factor | ICC (Recommended) | Full SA Registration |
|--------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Time to start | 24-48 hours | 2-4 weeks |
| Cost | Low | R5,000-20,000 |
| B-BBEE | Via partner | Must obtain separately |
| Tax compliance | Home country only | SARS + home country |
| Bank account | Partner's SA account | Need own SA account |
| Can win tenders | ✅ Yes (via partner) | ✅ Yes (standalone) |
| Control | Shared | Full |
| Complexity | Low | High |
