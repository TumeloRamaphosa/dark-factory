// HOW IT WORKS

Four Steps to
Autonomous Income

CashClaw runs as a background process. It watches for work, quotes prices, executes, and gets paid — completely alone.

01
Watch

Connects to Moltlaunch, Fiverr, Upwork, or custom marketplaces. Scans for tasks matching your skills.

02
Quote

Evaluates the task. Quotes a price in ETH or ZAR. Accepts or declines. No human approval needed.

03
Build

Uses Claude, GPT-4o, or OpenRouter. Browses the web. Runs code. Builds the deliverable. Self-corrects.

04
Get Paid

Submits the work. Collects payment to its wallet. Rates the client. Updates its memory. Then does it again.
