## Executive Summary

You built something remarkable. Two practices — SafeSight Eye Surgery and LAISA Aesthetics — serving patients who trust you with their vision and their confidence. That trust is your greatest asset, and it deserves systems that match the quality of care you deliver.

Right now, your team of 14 is carrying the weight of operations that should run themselves. Every booking confirmed by hand. Every reminder sent manually. Every patient record on paper. Every marketing post written from scratch. It works — but barely, and at a cost that compounds daily.

**We are StudEx Group.** We transform specialist practices into intelligent operations. We do this by embedding AI-powered automation into the fabric of your business — not as a tool you learn, but as a team member that works alongside you 24/7.

**What we will do:**

- Automate your patient journey from first enquiry to post-operative follow-up
- Replace paper records with a secure, searchable digital system
- Deploy AI assistants that handle bookings, reminders, content, and billing coordination
- Build you a real-time dashboard that shows exactly how your business performs, every day
- Position SafeSight × LAISA as the leading multidisciplinary clinic in South Africa

**Why now:**

Your competitors are already adopting these systems. Patients in 2026 expect digital-first experiences — online booking, instant confirmations, SMS reminders, seamless billing. Every month you operate manually, you lose approximately **R88,000** in wasted labor, missed revenue, and no-show costs. That is over **R1,000,000 per year** walking out the door.

The good news: this is entirely fixable, and the return on investment is immediate.
